Ask Us on WhatsApp →
Is there any hidden brokerage fee? +

No. Unwize charges zero hidden brokerage — the price you see on the listing is exactly what you pay to the owner.

How do I book a PG or flat? +

Just filter listings by your budget, location, and preference, then tap "Contact on WhatsApp" on any listing to chat directly with our team and confirm your stay.

What does the "Verified" badge mean? +

Verified stays have been personally visited and checked by the Unwize team for safety, cleanliness, and accurate photos before being listed.

Do you have separate PGs for girls? +

Yes, use the Preference filter to view Girls-only or Boys-only PGs near your college, each with dedicated security and amenities.
